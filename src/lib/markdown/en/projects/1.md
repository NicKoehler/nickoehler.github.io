## **Brawlhalla Stats** <sup>_[(Source)](https://github.com/NicKoehler/Brawlhalla-Stats)_</sup>

An application written in **Kotlin** for native Android, designed to display various statistics for the well-known video game [Brawlhalla](https://www.brawlhalla.com).

Currently, the application boasts more than 300 active installations (from the Play Store).
